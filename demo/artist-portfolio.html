About the artist

Mara Lindqvist (b. 1985, Malmö) studied painting at the Glasgow School of Art and has worked from a converted warehouse studio in Porto since 2018. Her practice moves between large-format oil landscapes and small monotype studies, both built from the same restrained, warm-grey palette punctuated by single notes of heat — ash, ember, rust.

Her work has been described as "geology painted from memory" — landscapes that are less observed than reconstructed, built up in thin oil layers over months. She teaches a yearly monotype intensive each autumn and accepts a limited number of private commissions.
